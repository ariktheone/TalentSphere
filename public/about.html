Our Journey

The milestones that shaped TalentSphere

2018

Inception

TalentSphere begins as a startup in San Francisco, born from the recognized need for more intuitive HR technology.

2019

First Product Launch

We release our initial recruitment management platform, helping small to medium businesses streamline their hiring processes.

2020

Pandemic Pivot

Rapidly developed remote work and digital onboarding features to support organizations during the global pandemic.

2022

Enterprise Expansion

Launched comprehensive talent management suite, expanding our reach to large enterprises and global organizations.

Growth & Expansion

We expanded to international markets and added AI-driven analytics to our platform, helping clients gain deeper workforce insights.

2023

Future Forward
